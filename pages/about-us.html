Our Story

How ShopVerse became a global e-commerce leader

From Startup to Global Platform

ShopVerse was founded in 2015 by a small team of e-commerce enthusiasts who believed online shopping could be more personal, accessible, and trustworthy. What started as a small operation with just 50 vendors and a few hundred products has grown into a global marketplace with over 15,000 verified vendors offering millions of products.

Our journey wasn't always smooth—we faced challenges common to many startups. But our unwavering commitment to creating a platform that benefits both shoppers and sellers equally helped us overcome these hurdles and steadily grow our presence around the world.

Today, ShopVerse connects millions of shoppers with trusted vendors across 120 countries, but we still maintain the customer-first philosophy that guided us from day one.

2015

ShopVerse Founded

ShopVerse launches with 50 vendors and a vision to create a more personalized online shopping experience.

2017

International Expansion

ShopVerse expands shipping to 35 countries and introduces multi-currency support.

2019

1 Million Customer Milestone

ShopVerse celebrates serving over 1 million customers and launches the vendor verification program.

2021

Mobile App Launch

ShopVerse releases its mobile app, making shopping on-the-go easier and more convenient.

2023

Sustainable Shopping Initiative

Introduction of eco-friendly shipping options and carbon offset program for all deliveries.

2025

Global Reach

ShopVerse now serves customers in 120 countries with 15,000+ trusted vendors and counting.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At ShopVerse, we believe that commerce and environmental responsibility can go hand in hand. We're committed to reducing our carbon footprint and promoting sustainable business practices across our entire platform.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

We're transitioning to 100% recyclable or biodegradable packaging materials by 2026.

Renewable Energy

Our offices and data centers are powered by 80% renewable energy, with a goal of 100% by 2027.

Carbon Offset Program

For every order shipped, we contribute to reforestation projects around the world.
