Return & Refund Policy Overview

At ShopVerse, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not entirely happy, we're here to help. This policy outlines our return and refund procedures to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

We have designed our return policy to be fair and transparent, giving you peace of mind when shopping with us. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations when returning products purchased from ShopVerse.

This Return & Refund Policy applies to all products purchased directly from ShopVerse website, mobile applications, and physical retail locations (if applicable). Different policies may apply for items purchased from third-party sellers on our marketplace.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, please make sure the following conditions are met:

  • Item must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • Item must be in the original packaging
  • Item must include all accessories, tags, and documentation
  • You must have the receipt or proof of purchase

Items that have been used, worn, altered, or damaged by customer handling may not be eligible for return.

For defective or damaged items received:

  • Report damaged or defective items within 48 hours of receipt
  • Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • Extended return window of 90 days for manufacturing defects
  • No return shipping costs for damaged or defective items
  • Option for replacement or refund

For certain electronics and appliances, manufacturer warranty may apply instead of our standard return policy. Please check the product documentation for specific warranty information.

For digital products and subscriptions:

  • Digital downloads are generally non-refundable once accessed or downloaded
  • E-books, digital codes, and software are non-returnable after the code is revealed or redeemed
  • Subscription services may be cancelled within 14 days if not used
  • Streaming content may be eligible for refund if technical issues prevented access
  • Digital gift cards are non-refundable

Please contact our customer service team for issues with digital products, as special handling may be required.

Return Process

We've simplified our return process to make it as convenient as possible. Please follow these steps to return an item:

1

Initiate Your Return

Log in to your ShopVerse account, navigate to your orders, and select "Return Item" next to the product you wish to return. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team.

2

Fill Out Return Form

Complete the return form, indicating your reason for return and your preferred resolution (refund or exchange). You'll receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.

3

Package Your Return

Carefully pack the item in its original packaging with all included accessories and documentation. Include the RMA number or return form in the package.

4

Ship Your Return

Use the provided return shipping label (if applicable) or send the package to our returns center. We recommend using a traceable shipping method.

5

Return Processing

Once we receive your return, we'll inspect the item and notify you of the status. Processing typically takes 3-5 business days from receipt.

Return Shipping Options

Doorstep Pickup

Available in select locations for orders over $50. Schedule a pickup and our carrier will collect the package from your doorstep.

Fee: $5.99 (Free for Premium)

Drop-Off Points

Print your return label and drop off your package at any of our authorized return locations nationwide.

Fee: $3.99 (Free for defective items)

Self-Ship

Ship the item back using your preferred carrier. Remember to include your RMA number on the package.

Fee: Varies by carrier

Return shipping fees will be deducted from your refund unless the item was damaged, defective, or incorrectly shipped. Premium members enjoy free return shipping on all eligible items.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene, safety, and other reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

Personal Care Items

Opened cosmetics, toiletries, grooming products, and hygiene products

Intimate Apparel

Underwear, swimwear, and intimate clothing items (if opened/worn)

Earphones & Headphones

In-ear headphones/earbuds if opened or with broken seal

Digital Media

Opened DVDs, CDs, software, digital downloads, and subscription codes

Gift Cards

Physical and digital gift cards (except where required by law)

Food Items

Perishable goods, food items, and consumables (unless spoiled upon receipt)

Customized Products

Made-to-order and personalized items (except for defects)

Final Sale Items

Items clearly marked as "Final Sale," "Non-Returnable," or "As-Is"

If you're unsure whether an item is returnable, please check the product description page or contact our customer service before making your purchase.

Damaged or Defective Items

We take quality seriously at ShopVerse. If you receive a damaged or defective item, please follow these steps:

1

Document the Damage

Take clear photos of the damaged item and packaging before opening completely. This includes the outer packaging, inner packaging, and the item itself.

2

Report Within 48 Hours

Contact our customer service team within 48 hours of receiving the damaged item. Provide your order number, description of the damage, and the photos you've taken.

3

Choose Resolution

Select whether you'd prefer a replacement item or a full refund. For defective items, we may request additional information to help identify the issue.

4

Return Shipping

We'll provide a prepaid return label for the damaged item. For large or heavy items, we may arrange doorstep pickup at no cost to you.

Types of Product Issues

Shipping Damage

Damage that occurred during transportation of the item:

  • Crushed, dented, or broken packaging
  • Water damage or exposure to elements
  • Product broken or damaged inside intact packaging
  • Missing components due to package tampering

Resolution: Immediate replacement or full refund, including shipping costs. Report within 48 hours of delivery.

Manufacturing Defect

Issues with the product quality or function:

  • Product doesn't function as described
  • Missing parts or components
  • Structural defects or flaws
  • Electrical or mechanical failure

Resolution: Replacement, repair, or full refund. Extended 90-day window for reporting manufacturing defects.

Incorrect Item

When you receive an item different from what you ordered:

  • Different product than what was purchased
  • Wrong size, color, or variation
  • Wrong quantity delivered
  • Missing components specific to your order

Resolution: Immediate correct replacement shipped with expedited delivery or full refund with free return shipping.

ShopVerse Guarantee: We stand behind every product we sell. If you receive a damaged or defective item, we promise to make it right with minimal hassle and maximum speed.

International Returns

For customers returning items from outside the country of purchase, please note the following special instructions:

International Return Process

  • Contact customer service before sending any international return
  • Obtain pre-approval and a special international return authorization
  • Clearly mark the package as "RETURNED GOODS" or "MERCHANDISE RETURN"
  • Include all customs documentation provided by our customer service team
  • Keep proof of shipping and tracking information

International returns may be subject to customs duties, taxes, and processing fees in the destination country. These fees are the responsibility of the customer and are not refundable by ShopVerse.

International Return Options

Standard International Return

Ship the item back using your preferred international carrier. Refund processed after receipt and inspection.

Customer pays return shipping

Keep & Refund (Small Items)

For small, low-value items, we may issue a refund without requiring return shipment.

Available case-by-case

Local Return Centers

Return to our local partner centers in select countries for expedited processing.

Available in UK, EU, Australia & Canada

International Refund Processing

International returns typically take longer to process than domestic returns:

  • International shipping time: 5-21 business days
  • Customs clearance: 1-5 business days
  • Return processing: 3-5 business days
  • Refund processing: 3-5 business days
  • International banking processing: 3-10 business days

Total time from shipping to refund may take 15-45 business days depending on your location and local banking systems.

Gift Returns

If you received an item as a gift and wish to return it, we have special procedures to maintain privacy and provide you with options:

Gift Return Options

Gift Receipt Return

If you have a gift receipt, you can return the item without notifying the purchaser. You'll receive store credit for the item's purchase price.

Gift Exchange

Exchange the gift for another item of equal value. We won't notify the gift giver, and you can choose a different size, color, or model.

Gift Card Refund

Return the gift and receive a ShopVerse gift card for the purchase amount. The gift giver will not be notified of the return.

Without a gift receipt, you'll need the order number or the email address of the purchaser to process your return. The refund will be issued as store credit only, not to the original payment method.

How to Return a Gift

  1. Visit our online returns center and select "Return a Gift"
  2. Enter the order number from your gift receipt or packing slip
  3. If you don't have the order number, you'll need the purchaser's email address
  4. Select the item(s) you wish to return and your preferred refund method
  5. Print the provided return shipping label and return instructions
  6. Package the item(s) securely and drop off at the designated shipping location

Privacy Guarantee

We respect your privacy during gift returns. The gift giver will not receive any notification about the return unless you specifically request it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about returns and refunds

How do I start a return?

To initiate a return, log in to your ShopVerse account and go to your order history. Select the order containing the item you wish to return, then click "Return Item." Follow the prompts to complete the return request. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team at returns@shopverse.com or call 1-888-RETURNS with your order number ready.

How long do I have to return an item?

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. However, the return window varies by product category: clothing and accessories (30 days), electronics (30 days unopened, 15 days if opened), beauty products (14 days, unopened only), and food items (7 days, unopened non-perishable only). For holiday purchases made between November 1st and December 31st, the return period is extended until January 31st.

Will I have to pay for return shipping?

Return shipping fees vary depending on the reason for return. If you're returning an item because it was damaged, defective, or incorrectly shipped, we'll cover the return shipping costs. For returns due to change of mind or other non-product issues, a return shipping fee will be deducted from your refund. ShopVerse Premium members enjoy free return shipping on all eligible items.

How long will it take to receive my refund?

After we receive your return, it typically takes 1-2 days for inspection, then 2-3 days for the refund to be processed. The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your payment method: credit/debit cards (5-10 business days), PayPal (3-5 business days), store credit (1-2 business days), or bank transfers (5-7 business days). During high volume periods, processing may take slightly longer.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, you can exchange eligible items for a different size, color, or model. During the return process, select "Exchange" rather than "Return for Refund" and specify the details of the item you'd like instead. If the exchange item costs more than your original purchase, you'll need to pay the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference to your original payment method.

What items cannot be returned?

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned: opened cosmetics and toiletries, underwear and swimwear (if opened/worn), opened in-ear headphones or earbuds, opened DVDs/software, digital downloads, gift cards, perishable goods, and personalized or custom-made items. Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" are also not eligible for return unless defective.

How do I return a gift?

To return a gift, visit our online returns center and select "Return a Gift." Enter the order number from your gift receipt or the gift giver's email address. You can choose to receive store credit for the purchase amount or exchange for a different item. The gift giver will not be notified of the return unless you specifically request it.

What if my item arrives damaged or defective?

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Take photos of the damaged item and packaging before opening completely. Contact our customer service team with your order number and the photos. We'll arrange a free return shipping method or replacement shipment. For large or heavy items, we may arrange doorstep pickup at no cost to you.
