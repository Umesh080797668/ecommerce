Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and support

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by going to the "Track Order" page and entering your order number and email address. You will also receive tracking information via email once your order has been shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. For detailed information, please visit our Returns & Refunds page.

How can I become a vendor on ShopVerse?

To become a vendor, visit our "Sell on ShopVerse" page and complete the vendor application form. Our team will review your application and contact you within 2-3 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for certain regions.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping times and costs vary by location. During checkout, you'll see available shipping options and estimated delivery times for your country.
