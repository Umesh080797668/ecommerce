Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our corporate program? Find your answers here

What is the minimum order quantity for corporate gift cards?

Our corporate gift card program starts at a minimum of 10 gift cards per order. Volume discounts begin at this level and increase with larger quantities.

Can we customize the gift cards with our company logo?

Yes, our custom branding option allows you to add your company logo, brand colors, and personalized messaging to both digital and physical gift cards. There is a one-time setup fee of $50 for this service.

How long does it take to receive physical gift cards?

Standard physical gift cards typically arrive within 5-7 business days after order approval. Custom-branded physical cards may require 7-10 business days. Rush shipping options are available for an additional fee.

Can we send gift cards to multiple recipients at once?

Yes, our bulk delivery system allows you to send gift cards to multiple recipients simultaneously. Simply upload a CSV file with recipient details through our corporate portal, and we'll handle the delivery.

Is there an expiration date on corporate gift cards?

No, ShopVerse gift cards never expire. Once purchased, the gift card balance remains available for the recipient to use at any time.

Can we set spending controls or restrictions?

Yes, our corporate program allows you to set certain restrictions such as category limitations (e.g., excluding alcohol or gift card purchases) and expiration dates for promotional cards if required by your program.

Do you offer international corporate gift cards?

Yes, we offer international gift card options for global teams and clients. Gift cards can be denominated in USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, and other major currencies. Please note that availability may vary by region.

What reporting and analytics are available?

Our corporate portal provides comprehensive reporting including delivery status, redemption rates, spending patterns, and remaining balances. Reports can be exported in CSV or PDF formats for your records.
