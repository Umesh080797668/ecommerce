Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our upcoming features

When exactly will these new features be available?

Our first major update is scheduled for June 15, 2025, with the Virtual Try-On Experience and AI Shopping Assistant. Additional features will be rolled out according to our timeline, with updates every month through October 2025.

Will I need to update my app to access these features?

Yes, to ensure the best experience, you'll need to update to the latest version of the ShopVerse app when the features are released. Web users will automatically see the new features without any action required.

How does the Virtual Try-On Experience work?

The Virtual Try-On Experience uses augmented reality (AR) technology. You can use your device's camera to see how clothes, accessories, or makeup would look on you in real-time. The feature works on compatible smartphones and tablets with cameras.

Is the AI Shopping Assistant available in all languages?

At launch, the AI Shopping Assistant will support English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese. We plan to add more languages in subsequent updates based on user demand and feedback.

How can I get early access to these features?

Sign up for notifications using the form on this page. Selected users will be invited to our beta testing program before the official launch. Beta testers will receive exclusive access and be able to provide valuable feedback to help us refine the features.
