Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ShopVerse Gift Cards

Do ShopVerse gift cards expire?

No, ShopVerse gift cards never expire. Once purchased, the gift card balance is available for the recipient to use at any time.

Can I use a gift card for partial payment?

Yes, gift cards can be used for partial payment. Any remaining balance on the gift card will be stored in the recipient's account for future purchases.

How quickly will digital gift cards be delivered?

Digital gift cards are delivered instantly unless you schedule them for future delivery. The recipient will receive an email with their gift card code and redemption instructions.

Can gift cards be returned or refunded?

Gift cards cannot be returned or refunded once purchased. However, if there's an issue with your gift card order, please contact our customer service within 48 hours of purchase.

How do I check the balance of a gift card?

You can check your gift card balance by visiting the "Gift Card Balance" page under your account settings or by contacting customer support with your gift card number.

Can I use gift cards internationally?

ShopVerse gift cards can be used internationally, but they are denominated in the currency they were purchased in. Currency conversion rates may apply if shopping in a different currency.
